Art Geeks

IIT Mandi

     

About



Art Geeks is the official art club of IIT Mandi. It is the heart of creativity in the college. In a short span, it has successfully established its identity in the college. Each year the club is responsible for new and innovative ways of promoting the art culture in the college.


Events

Vibgyor

Vibgyor is an annual 3 day long intra college art fest of IIT Mandi exclusively organized by Art Geeks to enable all students, faculty, and staff to showcase their artistic talents. It includes various art-related events :

  • Canvas
  • Sketching
  • Collage Making
  • Stone Painting
  • Painting


Exodia

Exodia is the annual cultural and technical fest of IIT Mandi. It have the events organised by all the technical and cultural society of IIT Mandi

ArtGeeks has an important contribution in Exodia trough the designing of various stuffs around the campus including 3D models,Photo Booth, Wall Painting....

It organises a number of events like:

  • Canvas Making
  • Doodle Making
  • Face Painting
  • Live Sketching
