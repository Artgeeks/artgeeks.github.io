About




Art Geeks is the Art-Club of IIT Mandi. It is the heart of the creativity in the college. In a small phase it has successfully established its identity in the college Throughout the time it has involved in different activities including wall paintings, graffiti and many other exciting stuffs


Events


Vibgyor

Vibgyor is the annual intracollege fest of IIT Mandi exclucively organised by the ArtGeeks for the encouaging of involvement of the students in art related activities.

It include a number of events which include:

  • Canvas
  • Sketching
  • Collage Making
  • Stone Painting
  • Painting

Exodia

Exodia is the annual cultural and technical fest of IIT Mandi. It have the events organised by all the technical and cultural society of IIT Mandi

ArtGeeks has an important contribution in Exodia trough the designing of various stuffs around the campus including 3D models,Photo Booth, Wall Painting....

It organises a number of events like:

  • Canvas Making
  • Doodle Making
