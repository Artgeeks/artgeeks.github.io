About

Art Geeks is the art club of the IIT Mandi. It is the heart of the creativity in the college. In a small phase it has successfully established its identity in the college

Throughout the time it has involved in different different activities including wall paintings, graffiti and many exciting stuffs

Events


Vibgyor

Vibgyor is the annual intracollege fest of IIT Mandi exclucively by the ArtGeeks for the encouagement of the involvement of the students in the art related activities.

It include a number of events which include -

  • Canvas
  • Sketching
  • Collage Making
  • Stone Painting
  • Painting

@Exodia

Exodia is the annual cultural and technical fest of IIT Mandi. It have the events by all the technical and cultural society of IIT Mandi

ArtGeeks has an important contribution toward Exodia trough the designing of various stuffs around the campus including 3D models,Photo Booth, Wall Painting....

It organises a number of events like -

  • Canvas Making
  • Doodle Making
